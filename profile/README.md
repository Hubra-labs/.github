### What we build

We make tools that give Solana users superpowers — staking, DeFi, vaults, analytics, and everything in between. All self-custody. All open source.

**🏦 [SolanaHub](https://github.com/Hubra-labs/SolanaHub)** — The all-in-one Solana dashboard. Stake, swap, track, and manage your portfolio in one place. Built for degens & maxis.

**📊 [Hubra](https://github.com/Hubra-labs/hubra-home)** — Real-time Solana token analytics. Charts, market data, and portfolio tracking with a clean, fast interface.

**⚖️ [Vaults Rebalancer](https://github.com/Hubra-labs/vaults-rebalancer)** — Automated vault rebalancing engine for DeFi yield optimization.

**🔥 [SolBlaze Flux](https://github.com/Hubra-labs/solblaze-flux)** — Identify underperforming validators and redelegate your stake. Better yield. Better decentralization.

**🚰 [Devnet Faucet](https://github.com/Hubra-labs/devnet-faucet)** — Airdrop SOL, SPL tokens, and NFTs on devnet. Built for developers who ship fast.

**📚 [Solana Staking Lib](https://github.com/Hubra-labs/solana-staking-lib)** — Drop-in staking SDK for validators to integrate in-app staking.
