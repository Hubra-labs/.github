### About

Hubra is building the next generation of DeFi infrastructure on Solana. We deploy autonomous AI agents that manage on-chain capital — from liquidity provisioning and yield optimization to risk hedging and portfolio rebalancing.

No more manual monitoring. No more chasing yields across fragmented protocols. One intelligent system, fully self-custody.

**Building on Solana since 2020.**

### What we're shipping

**⚡ [Hubra App](https://hubra.app)** — The agentic DeFi wallet. AI-powered strategies, real-time analytics, and autonomous portfolio management.

**⚖️ [Vaults Rebalancer](https://github.com/Hubra-labs/vaults-rebalancer)** — Automated vault rebalancing engine for DeFi yield optimization.

**🔥 [SolBlaze Flux](https://github.com/Hubra-labs/solblaze-flux)** — Identify underperforming validators and redelegate your stake. Better yield, better decentralization.

**🚰 [Devnet Faucet](https://github.com/Hubra-labs/devnet-faucet)** — Airdrop SOL, SPL tokens, and NFTs on devnet. Built for developers who ship fast.

**📚 [Staking SDK](https://github.com/Hubra-labs/solana-staking-lib)** — Drop-in staking library for validators to integrate in-app staking.

**📖 [Docs](https://github.com/Hubra-labs/docs)** — Protocol documentation and guides.
